Our Team

Alex Waters

Founder & CEO | 6 years | Ext: 101

Alex Waters, a marine biologist and eco-entrepreneur, is the visionary behind Run for the Oceans. With a Ph.D. in Marine Ecology from the University of California, Santa Barbara, Alex spent over a decade researching the devastating effects of plastic pollution on marine ecosystems before founding the company in 2018. Alex's innovative approach to turning ocean waste into high-performance running shoes has been a game-changer in the sustainability industry. Beyond her scientific expertise, Alex is a charismatic leader known for her ability to inspire global movements, such as the world’s largest ocean cleanup initiative in 2019. When she's not working, Alex enjoys marathon running, scuba diving, and mentoring the next generation of eco-conscious entrepreneurs.

Samantha Lee

Sustainability Director | 5 years | Ext: 102

Samantha Lee brings a wealth of experience in sustainable development and environmental advocacy to her role as Sustainability Director. With a Master’s degree in Environmental Policy from Yale University, Samantha oversees the company’s green initiatives, ensuring every aspect of production is eco-friendly. She spearheaded the development of the Tide Trek trail-running shoes, which became the first footwear in the industry made entirely from post-consumer ocean plastics. Samantha’s passion for sustainability extends to her personal life, where she’s an avid advocate for zero-waste living and serves as a guest lecturer at environmental conferences worldwide.

Marcus Rivera

Design Lead | 4 years | Ext: 103

Marcus Rivera is the creative force behind Run for the Oceans' innovative and stylish footwear. A graduate of the Rhode Island School of Design, Marcus blends his passion for cutting-edge fashion with a commitment to sustainability. His designs for the Wave Runner and EcoSprint Pro lines have earned international acclaim for their aesthetic appeal and performance-enhancing features. Marcus is constantly pushing boundaries, exploring new ways to incorporate recycled materials into functional designs. Outside of work, he channels his creativity into art installations that highlight the global plastic crisis and its impact on coastal communities.

Rachel Gomez

Marketing Manager | 3 years | Ext: 104

Rachel Gomez, with her background in digital marketing and brand strategy, has been instrumental in building Run for the Oceans' global presence. Holding a Bachelor’s degree in Marketing from New York University, Rachel specializes in storytelling that resonates with eco-conscious consumers. She led the viral marketing campaign for the Wave Runner 1.0, which sold out within hours of its release and solidified the brand as a leader in sustainable footwear. Rachel’s innovative approach includes partnerships with influencers, organizing community cleanup events, and leveraging social media to inspire change. A passionate environmentalist, Rachel enjoys hiking, paddleboarding, and organizing beach cleanups in her spare time.
